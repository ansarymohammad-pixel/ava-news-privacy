Privacy Policy for AVA Intelligence Apps

This Privacy Policy applies to AVA Intelligence applications, including AVA News and Hafez Sky. It explains how we access, process, use, and protect user and device information in connection with our mobile apps and related backend services.

Effective Date

Effective date: April 29, 2026

Apps Covered by This Policy

  • AVA News - a headline analysis and source transparency app.
  • Hafez Sky - a poetry, astronomy, and symbolic reading app.

Information We May Process

AVA News

  • Text entered by the user for headline or content analysis
  • News source or publisher information used for transparency or context
  • Technical request and device information required for app operation, reliability, and security
  • Limited diagnostic or error information used to maintain the service

Hafez Sky

  • Optional nickname entered by the user
  • Optional birth month and birth date entered by the user
  • Mood, question, or intention entered by the user
  • Selected app language and reading preferences
  • Optional approximate or precise location data, when the user grants permission
  • Technical request and device information required for app operation, reliability, and security

How We Use Information

  • Provide app features and generate results based on user input
  • Operate backend services that support app responses
  • Maintain, secure, debug, and improve app performance and reliability
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Location Data

Hafez Sky may request location permission to provide location-aware sky or weather context. Location access is optional. If permission is not granted, the app may still function with more general results.

Backend and Third-Party Services

AVA Intelligence apps may send user-submitted data to AVA Intelligence backend services in order to generate results and operate app features.

Certain features may also rely on third-party infrastructure or APIs, including hosting providers, weather providers, and public information sources.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only as reasonably necessary to provide app functionality, maintain security, resolve technical issues, or comply with legal obligations.

Data Retention

We aim to retain processed data only for as long as reasonably necessary for app functionality, reliability, security, troubleshooting, legal compliance, and service improvement.

Children's Privacy

AVA Intelligence apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect information processed through our apps. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date shown above.

Contact: For privacy questions, support requests, or app-related concerns, contact us at support@avaintelligent.info.
