News Source Transparency

AVA News may display or analyze headlines from third-party publishers and public news sources. The app is not the original publisher of those third-party articles.

  • Headlines shown in the app identify the source or publisher where available.
  • Users can tap source items in the app to open the original publisher or source page where available.
  • Third-party publisher names remain visible in the app to help users understand where content comes from.
  • AVA News is designed for analysis and source comparison, not for replacing original publishers.
