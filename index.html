Privacy Policy for AVA News

AVA News provides headline analysis features designed to help users evaluate news content using credibility, rhetoric, source transparency, and risk indicators.

Effective Date

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Information We Process

AVA News may process the following information:

  • Text entered by the user for headline analysis
  • Technical request data required for app operation
  • Limited diagnostic information for reliability and security
  • News source or publisher information used for source transparency

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Analyze submitted headlines
  • Return credibility, rhetoric, and risk-related insights
  • Show source and publisher context where available
  • Maintain, secure, and improve app performance

Backend and Third-Party Services

AVA News may send user-submitted headline text to our backend service in order to generate analysis results. The app may also rely on third-party infrastructure or APIs, such as hosting providers and news-related services, to function properly.

AVA News may display or analyze headlines from third-party publishers and public news sources. AVA News is not the original publisher of third-party articles.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Submitted text is used only for providing app functionality, maintaining the service, and improving reliability.

Data Retention

We aim to keep processed data only as long as necessary for app functionality, reliability, security, and legal compliance.

Children's Privacy

AVA News is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact: If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, app support, or content-related concerns, contact us at support@avaintelligent.info or visit our Contact page.
