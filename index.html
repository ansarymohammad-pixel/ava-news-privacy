Privacy Policy for AVA Intelligence Apps

This Privacy Policy applies to AVA Intelligence applications, including AVA News and Hafez Sky. It explains how we access, process, use, and protect user and device information in connection with our mobile apps and related backend services.

Effective Date

Effective date: April 29, 2026

Apps Covered by This Policy

  • AVA News - a headline analysis and source transparency app.
  • Hafez Sky - a poetry, astronomy, and symbolic reading app.

If a future AVA Intelligence app links to this page, that app must also be clearly identified in the Play listing or app experience and handled in a way consistent with this policy.

Information We May Process

AVA News

  • Text entered by the user for headline or content analysis
  • News source or publisher information used for transparency or context
  • Technical request and device information required for app operation, reliability, and security
  • Limited diagnostic or error information used to maintain the service

Hafez Sky

  • Optional nickname entered by the user
  • Optional birth month and birth date entered by the user
  • Mood, question, or intention entered by the user
  • Selected app language and reading preferences
  • Optional approximate or precise location data, when the user grants permission
  • Technical request and device information required for app operation, reliability, and security

How We Use Information

We may use information processed through AVA Intelligence apps to:

  • Provide headline analysis, symbolic reading, weather, or astronomy-related features
  • Generate app results based on user input and app functionality
  • Operate backend services that support app responses
  • Maintain, secure, monitor, debug, and improve app performance and reliability
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations and enforce our terms or policies

Location Data

Hafez Sky may request device location permission in order to provide location-aware sky or weather context. Location access is optional. If a user does not grant location permission, the app may still function with more general or non-location-based results.

We do not request location permission for unrelated advertising or marketing purposes.

Backend and Third-Party Services

AVA Intelligence apps may send user-submitted data to AVA Intelligence backend services in order to generate results, operate app features, or maintain service reliability.

Certain features may also rely on third-party infrastructure or APIs, such as hosting providers, weather providers, analytics-free diagnostic infrastructure, or public information sources. For example, Hafez Sky may use third-party weather data providers to support weather-related context.

AVA News may display, analyze, or reference headlines or public publisher information from third-party sources. AVA Intelligence is not the original publisher of third-party news content.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide app functionality through service providers or infrastructure partners
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, or resolve technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations or lawful requests

Data Retention and Deletion

We aim to retain processed data only for as long as reasonably necessary for app functionality, reliability, security, troubleshooting, legal compliance, and service improvement.

If you have a privacy or deletion request related to data submitted through an AVA Intelligence app, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

AVA Intelligence apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through one of our apps, please contact us.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information processed through our apps. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date shown above. Continued use of our apps after an update means the updated policy will apply, subject to applicable law.

Contact: If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, privacy requests, app support, or content-related concerns, contact us at support@avaintelligent.info or visit our Contact page.
