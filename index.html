Privacy Policy for AVA News

Effective date: April 14, 2026

AVA News provides headline analysis features designed to help users evaluate news content using credibility, rhetoric, and risk indicators.

Information We Process

AVA News may process the following information:

  • Text entered by the user for headline analysis
  • Technical request data required for app operation
  • Limited diagnostic information for reliability and security

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Analyze submitted headlines
  • Return credibility, rhetoric, and risk-related insights
  • Maintain, secure, and improve app performance

Backend and Third-Party Services

AVA News may send user-submitted headline text to our backend service in order to generate analysis results. The app may also rely on third-party infrastructure or APIs, such as hosting providers and news-related services, to function properly.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Submitted text is used only for providing app functionality and maintaining the service.

Data Retention

We aim to keep processed data only as long as necessary for app functionality, reliability, and security.

Children’s Privacy

AVA News is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.
